Privacy Policy

Introduction

At MData, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our data marketplace platform.

Data Collection

We collect the following information to facilitate transactions and provide our services:

  • Name and email address (for account creation)
  • Payment details (processed securely via Razorpay)
  • Uploaded data metadata (for quality assessment)
  • Usage analytics (to improve our platform)

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Process payments and facilitate transactions
  • Verify the quality of metadata provided
  • Communicate important updates about your account
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share only necessary information with:

  • Razorpay - Our payment processor for transaction processing
  • Microsoft Azure - Our hosting and cloud storage provider

These partners are bound by strict data protection agreements.

Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged into your MData account
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze platform usage (anonymized)

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure HTTPS connections, and regular security audits to protect your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
